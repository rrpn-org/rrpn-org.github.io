**Forthcoming.** This publication is in progress.

When disaster-struck communities rebuild, they do so inside a web of state law that
was rarely written with recovery in mind. This framework will examine where state
preemption of local land-use authority helps rebuilding communities — and where it
hurts them — and propose best practices for legislators, local governments, and
residents navigating that balance.
